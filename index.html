Favourite Food

The creamy coconut chutney adds a refreshing, mild sweetness that balances the flavors beautifully. Paired with it, the spicy, tangy sambar—rich with tamarind, lentils, and fresh vegetables—brings warmth and a burst of taste in every bite. Together, they create a delightful harmony of textures and spices, making each mouthful pure joy. It’s not just food—it’s happiness served on a plate.
